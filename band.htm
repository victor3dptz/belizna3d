The Band

Peter Sadovnikov - the creator of the band (born in 1965). Has no professional musical education. Before the Belizna3D he worked in some bands in Saint Petersburg. His profession is computer engineering. His hobbies are reading English books and music.
Alexander Cherepuho (born in 1946) is professional drum player. In Saint Petersburg he is famous for his great skills. In 1994 he had a big problem with his health and that is why he had to leave the band for some time. But since 1995 he is constant participant of the band again.
Pavel Berezovsky (born in 1966) - he used to be raper. In 1989 he and Sergey W. Ivanov were participants of the band called "Hip-Hop Studio"(In Latvia).
Sergey W. Ivanov (born in 1958) is Belizna3D's poet. He has 2 books with his poems. He is also an artist. He has some pictures mainly about nature.

Lyona B.(born in 1954) is known as Big Lion. He is biker and he prefers not to speak about himself.
