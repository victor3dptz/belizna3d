History of the Band

The band Belizna3D was created in 1992 by Peter Sadovnikov. There are 5 musicants in the band. They are: Peter Sadovnikov(Vocals, Guitars, Percussion), Pavel Berezovsky (Guitars, Back vocals), Lyona B.(Bass Guitars), Sergey W. Ivanov (Keyboards, Drum programming) and Alexander Cherepuho (Drums).
The band has 5 albums: Smiths(1993), Catolik(1995), Istoria [History](1996), WWWorld(1998), and Roga i Kopyta [Horns and Hoofs](1999). The most well-known songs are Stena(The Wall), Wuthering Heights, Smiths, Musicians-Boys and others. The structure of the band was fixed. But one time Alexander Cherepuho left the band because of his health problems (he returned to Belizna3D now). Also there was a guest vocalist in the band (Gennady Vasyanovsky).
The first name of the band was Woodoodoo, but later (in 1993) they changed it to Belizna3D. The band visited some European Countries and some cities in Russia. All the musicians support different acts and play in concerts organised by different societies (like GreenPeace and WWF). All of the participants of the band try to express the idea of healhy way of life (without drugs, alcohol and smoking).
The records of the band are produced in a little number and they are considered to be rare. Now the band sing mainly in underground clubs of Saint Petersburg (in Russia).
